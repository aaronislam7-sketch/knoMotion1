Based on your project's creative enhancement philosophy, here are the key characteristics to analyze:

### 1. **Living, Breathing Motion** ‚ú®

**What to Look For:**
- **Ambient background elements** that create subtle, continuous motion
- Elements that feel "alive" rather than static between keyframes
- Organic movement that draws the eye without overwhelming content
- Depth created through layered motion at different speeds

**Questions:**
- Do ambient particles or floating shapes add depth?
- Is there continuous subtle motion even during "hold" moments?
- Does the background breathe with the content?

**Your Project Standard:**
- 12-20 ambient particles (opacity 0.15-0.4)
- Floating organic shapes
- Parallax depth layers

### 2. **Microdelights & Celebration Moments** üéâ

**What to Look For:**
- **Sparkles/particle bursts** that emphasize key moments
- Confetti or celebration effects for success states
- Small delightful surprises that reward viewer attention
- Effects that enhance emotional peaks

**Questions:**
- Do important reveals have sparkle/burst effects?
- Are there celebratory moments that feel rewarding?
- Do microdelights support the narrative arc?

**Your Project Standard:**
- Sparkles on reveals (6-10 particles)
- Confetti bursts on success (25-30 particles)
- Checkmarks, Lottie animations for key moments

---

### 3. **Professional Glow & Shimmer** ‚ú®

**What to Look For:**
- **Subtle glows** that pulse on important elements
- Sweeping shimmer effects that catch light
- Bloom effects that add premium feel
- Proper use of visual emphasis without distraction

**Questions:**
- Do titles/key elements have subtle glows?
- Is there a sweeping shine effect on important text?
- Does the glow pulse naturally (not too fast)?

**Your Project Standard:**
- Glow intensity: 6-12
- Pulse speed: 0.04-0.06 (slow, natural)
- Shimmer sweeps with golden/accent colors

- **Text that moves with physics** (wave, scatter, orbit)

- Bouncy, playful letter entrances
- Typography that feels dimensional

**Questions:**
- Does text entrance feel dynamic or static?
- Are there wave/ripple effects through words?
- Do letters have individual personality?

**Your Project Standard:**
- Kinetic wave (amplitude: 8px, frequency: 0.08)
- Character-by-character reveals
- Bouncy letter entrances

### 5. **Draw-On Effects** ‚úçÔ∏è

**What to Look For:**
- **Handwriting-style animations** (underlines, circles, highlights)
- Marker/pen effects that emphasize key text
- Drawing animations that feel natural
- Reveals that mimic analog creative tools

**Questions:**
- Are there underlines/circles that draw on?
- Does highlighting feel like a marker swipe?
- Is the timing natural (not too fast/slow)?

**Your Project Standard:**

### 6. **Liquid/Organic Shapes** üíß

**What to Look For:**
- **Blob shapes** that morph and flow
- Organic background elements with fluid motion
- Shapes that add depth without distraction
- Natural, flowing animations (not mechanical)

**Questions:**
- Are there organic blobs in the background?
- Do shapes morph smoothly?
- Is the motion natural and fluid?

**Your Project Standard:**
- Liquid blobs with 6+ control points
- Subtle wobble/morph
- Opacity: 0.15 for background elements

---

### 7. **Appropriate Restraint** üé®

**What to Look For:**
- **Effects that enhance, not overwhelm**
- Restraint matched to content type
- Clean, readable content at all times
- "Magic in moderation" principle

**Questions:**
- Can you still read all text easily?
- Do effects support or compete with content?
- Is there appropriate restraint for the context?

**Your Project Standard:**
- Hook templates: Energetic (20 particles, higher opacity)
- Explain templates: Balanced (15 particles, medium opacity)
- Reflect templates: Minimal (6-8 particles, 0.15 opacity)
- Content is ALWAYS king

---

### 8. **Production Quality Indicators** üé¨

**What to Look For:**
- **High bitrate/quality** encoding
- Smooth 60fps motion (no stutter)
- Clean renders (no artifacts)
- Professional color grading

**Technical Indicators:**
- Resolution: 1080p or higher
- Frame rate: 60fps
- Bitrate: 4+ Mbps
- Color depth: Proper contrast and saturation

### 9. **Timing & Pacing** ‚è±Ô∏è

**What to Look For:**
- **Natural timing** (not too rushed or slow)
- Staggered reveals (elements don't all appear at once)
- Hold moments for comprehension
- Smooth entrance/exit animations

**Questions:**
- Do elements stagger naturally (0.3-0.8s apart)?
- Is there time to read/comprehend?
- Are entrances/exits smooth (0.5-1s)?

**Your Project Standard:**
- Entrance: 0.5s
- Element interval: 0.3-0.8s
- Hold: 2-5s
- Exit: 0.5-1s

---

### 10. **Compositional Excellence** üìê

**What to Look For:**
- **Bold sizing** (not timid UI elements)
- Proper use of whitespace
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Elements use 90-95% of screen space

**Questions:**
- Are elements sized boldly (180-260px)?
- Is there proper whitespace/breathing room?
- Is the visual hierarchy clear?

**Your Project Standard:**
- Element sizes: 180-260px
- Use 90-95% of 1920x1080
- Top margin: 70-80px
- Side margins: 80-100px

---

### 11. **Color & Style Harmony** üé®

**What to Look For:**
- **Cohesive color palette** (2-3 accent colors)
- Effects use scene colors dynamically
- Professional gradients
- Appropriate contrast

**Questions:**
- Do all effects use the same accent colors?
- Is there a clear color hierarchy?
- Are gradients smooth and professional?

**Your Project Standard:**
- Effects use `[colors.accent, colors.accent2, colors.accent3]`
- Dynamic color matching
- Proper contrast ratios
